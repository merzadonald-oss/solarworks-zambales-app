1. INTRODUCTION

SolarWorks Zambales ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

a) Location Data
   We collect your device's location to calculate the optimal solar panel tilt and direction for your specific geographic area. This data is used solely to provide accurate solar energy recommendations and is not stored on our servers.

b) Name and Email Address
   We may collect your name and email address when you register or contact us for support. This information is used to identify your account and communicate with you regarding the App.

─────────────────────────────────────────────

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide and improve the App's features and functionality
- To calculate solar panel recommendations based on your location
- To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or inquiries
- To ensure the security and proper operation of the App

─────────────────────────────────────────────

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely within the SolarWorks Zambales app to provide you with our services.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Location data is processed on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Your name and email address, if provided, are stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.
